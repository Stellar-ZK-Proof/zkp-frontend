How it works

Private settlement, zero guesswork.

01

Commit privately

Amount, recipient and a random salt are hashed into a commitment. Only the hash goes on-chain — the values stay with you.

02

Generate ZK proof

A Groth16 proof is computed off-chain, proving the commitment is well-formed and the nullifier is fresh — without revealing inputs.

03

Settle on Stellar

The Soroban contract verifies the proof against the on-chain verifier key and marks the payment settled. Takes ~5 seconds.

04

Audit on demand

The sender can reveal the preimage to any auditor at any time, enabling full compliance without exposing data to the public ledger.
